A slide-over cart drawer for the Aura Luxury Chocolates store, showcasing the visual and tactile "craving-first" aesthetic even in the transactional layer.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Palette: Primary Dark Coffee (#3C2A21) for text, Secondary Milk Chocolate (#89523E) for accents, Background Light Brown/Cream (#F5EFE6) for the drawer. The backdrop overlay should be a blurred semi-transparent dark shade.
- Typography: Playfair Display for headers; Inter for body and details.
- Vibe: Craving-first interface, tactile feedback, visual richness, premium and elegant.
- Micro-interactions: Items in the cart should have hover effects.

**Page Structure:**
1. Background: A blurred, semi-transparent dark overlay showing the shop behind it.
2. The Drawer: Attached to the right side of the screen, taking up about 35% of the desktop width. Background: #F5EFE6.
3. Drawer Header: "Your Collection" in Playfair Display. A sleek "X" (close) button.
4. Cart Items: List of 2 items (e.g., Noir Ganache, Rosemary Honey). Each item row should have:
   - A small square thumbnail of the high-res image.
   - Title in Playfair, price in Inter.
   - A minimalist quantity selector (- 1 +).
   - A "Remove" text link (in #89523E).
5. Subtotal Section: 
   - Row for Subtotal: $8.25.
   - Row for Shipping: "Calculated at next step".
6. Checkout Action: A large, tactile "Proceed to Checkout" button filling the bottom width, using a Primary Coffee (#3C2A21) gradient.
7. Empty State (Optional subtle text): "Complimentary tasting notes included with every order." in italics at the absolute bottom.
