# Aura | Luxury Chocolate Design System

## 6. Design System Notes for Stitch Generation
**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Palette: Primary Dark Coffee (#3C2A21) for headers and text, Secondary Milk Chocolate (#89523E) for accent buttons and borders, Background Light Brown/Cream (#F5EFE6) for soft contrast/readability.
- Typography: Headers in Playfair Display (Serif), body in Inter (Sans-serif) 14px-16px.
- Styles: 
  - Micro-interactions: Cards should have 3D-scale effects on hover and reveal a "Quick Add" (+) button.
  - Layouts: Utilize split-screen asymmetric layouts: 60% high-quality "food porn" imagery, 40% elegant, minimal UI/text.
  - Vibe: Craving-first interface, tactile feedback, visual richness, premium and elegant.
